# PROMPTS DE ILUSTRACIONES — "Orbi y el Último Color del Universo"

Pensados para Midjourney, DALL·E 3, Leonardo o Ideogram.
Copia el **ESTILO BASE** al final de cada prompt para que TODAS las páginas
parezcan del mismo ilustrador (clave para un libro profesional).

---

## 🎨 ESTILO BASE (pégalo en cada imagen)
> children's picture book illustration, soft hand-painted gouache and watercolor
> texture, rounded friendly shapes, cinematic but cozy lighting, gentle grain,
> dreamy sci-fi, limited but glowing palette, wide storybook composition with
> room for text, consistent character design --ar 16:9 --style raw

**Personaje principal — Orbi:** niña pequeña de unos 6 años, piel morena, pelo
rizado oscuro y abundante, mono de mecánico amarillo con bolsillos, botas grandes.
**Chispa:** luciérnaga de hojalata, redondeada, con una lucecita cálida en el centro.

> Consejo: genera primero a Orbi y a Chispa solos, guarda esa imagen y úsala como
> referencia (`--cref` en Midjourney) en el resto de páginas para mantenerlos iguales.

---

### PORTADA
A curly-haired girl in a yellow mechanic jumpsuit standing on a tiny grey moon,
holding a small tin firefly that glows warm; behind her a vast space slowly
filling with ribbons of color; title space at the top. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 1 — El universo se apaga
A wide grey galaxy losing its colors, the red, blue and yellow drifting away like
frightened birds into darkness, melancholic and quiet. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 2 — Orbi y Chispa
A small grey moon with a cozy scrap-metal house; a curly-haired girl with pockets
full of bolts smiles beside a tiny glowing tin firefly. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 3 — El cohete de lata
The girl builds a dog-sized rocket out of an old can, three springs and bits of
junk, eyes full of determination, blueprints scattered around. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 4 — El gigante triste
A lonely ash-grey planet with tall silent mountains; a huge gentle giant sits
alone, sad, everything colorless around him. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 5 — La bufanda (nace el naranja)
The little girl gives her scarf to the giant and listens to him; he smiles, and
the mountains burst into warm orange light spreading across the scene. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 6 — El secreto
Close-up of the girl's wondering face, a small orange glow reflected in her eyes,
realizing colors awaken with kindness. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 7 — El mar seco
A cracked dry planet, an old wise jellyfish searching for water for her babies,
dusty and grey. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 8 — El manantial (nace el azul)
The girl and the tin firefly dig and find a hidden spring; water fills the sea and
glows a brilliant blue, jellyfish babies dancing happily. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 9 — El asteroide olvidado
A small bare asteroid where many ragged children have nothing to eat or play with,
muted colors, tender mood. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 10 — Compartir (nace el amarillo)
The girl shares her snack and bolts, the tin firefly lights up for everyone;
children laugh and a warm golden-yellow sun-glow fills the asteroid. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 11 — El secreto revelado
The girl floating in space surrounded by softly glowing colored orbs, each born
from a kind act, magical and warm. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 12 — La estela de colores
The little tin rocket trailing a rainbow ribbon across the sky, other children in
their own tiny ships joining the journey. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 13 — El universo renace
A full galaxy glowing again with reds, greens, violets and gold, hugs and sharing
shown as colorful light, joyful and vast. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. 14 — Tú guardas un color
A child looking up at a star-filled colorful sky from a window, one warm color
glowing softly in their chest, hopeful and tender. [+ ESTILO BASE]

### Pág. final — Cáritas
Soft warm scene of diverse hands sharing a small glowing light, gentle and
hopeful, lots of empty space, calm palette. [+ ESTILO BASE]
